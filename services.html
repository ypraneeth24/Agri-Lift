Our soil testing service can be requested through our mobile app or website. Simply:

  1. Create an account or login
  2. Navigate to "Services" and select "Soil Testing"
  3. Enter your farm details and preferred testing date
  4. Complete the payment

One of our technicians will visit your farm on the scheduled date to collect samples.

We offer a wide variety of seeds including cereals, pulses, vegetables, fruits, and ornamental plants. All our seeds are certified, tested for quality, and have guaranteed germination rates. We specialize in climate-adapted varieties that are suitable for different regions and growing conditions.

Our smart farming system uses IoT sensors placed throughout your farm to monitor key parameters like soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and more. These sensors send real-time data to our cloud platform, which you can access through our mobile app or web dashboard.

The system uses AI to analyze this data and provide actionable insights, such as when to irrigate, fertilize, or take pest control measures. For irrigation, the system can be configured to automatically activate your irrigation equipment when moisture levels fall below optimal thresholds.
